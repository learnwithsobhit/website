About Us

We started operations in 1985 strategically located in MIDC Taloja. Shakti Gas has come a long way since with a strong customer base in MIDC Taloja and in the catchment area around Navi Mumbai. Shakti Gas aims to deliver Industrial and Specialty gases to a wide range of customers, and we are committed to providing excellent service that is safe, reliable, and cost-effective.
